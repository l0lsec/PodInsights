Option 1: Enable Profile Access (Recommended)

Add the "Sign In with LinkedIn using OpenID Connect" product to your LinkedIn Developer App:

  1. Go to LinkedIn Developer Portal
  2. Select your app
  3. Click on the "Products" tab
  4. Find "Sign In with LinkedIn using OpenID Connect" and click "Request access"
  5. Accept the terms and wait for approval (usually instant)
  6. Once approved, disconnect and reconnect your LinkedIn account

Your LinkedIn Member ID is a unique identifier. How do I find my Member ID?
To find your LinkedIn Member ID:
  1. Go to your LinkedIn profile
  2. Look at the URL: linkedin.com/in/your-username/
  3. Click on "More" → "Save to PDF" to get a PDF version
  4. Or use the LinkedIn API Explorer tool
  5. The Member ID looks like: urn:li:person:ABC123xyz
  6. Enter just the alphanumeric part: ABC123xyz

Alternative: Your Member ID may also be visible in network requests when viewing your own posts on LinkedIn (check browser developer tools).
